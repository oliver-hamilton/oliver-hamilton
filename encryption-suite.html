Encryption Suite

Technologies

  • The web app was written entirely using HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
  • The goal was to gain a better understanding of the various encryption algorithms that I had learned about during my university studies by implementing them myself. These include simple algorithms, such as XOR encryption and transposition ciphers, as well as complex, widely used algoirthms such as DES and AES.
  • By implementing these algorithms in their entirety, I gained a full understanding of the intricacies underlying them.
